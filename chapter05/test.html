Did you know your browser can give away information without your permission? The following is information about your computer system.

Object property: Description: Information reported by your browser:
screen.colorDepth Your screen's bit depth
navigator.platform Your operating system
navigator.userAgent Your browser compatibility
screen.width Width of your display
screen.height Height of your display
navigator.geolocation Your current location

A spooky example of how your location can be found without your consent.
